Overview

Purpose

Our white water rafting website promotes adventures, provides essential trip info, and allows easy online

booking. It builds trust with customers, and showcases our safety and professionalism.

With clear details and a mobile-friendly design, it streamlines customer interaction and attracts

more bookings. This site helps us grow, save time on inquiries, and stand out in a competitive market by

offering a thrilling, trustworthy rafting experience online.

Audience

Visitors to our white water rafting website will be mature adventure-seekers, outdoor

enthusiasts, tourists, and families looking for fun and memorable experiences. Many will

be first-time rafters, so the website needs to feel welcoming, informative, and easy to

navigate. We will design with these users in mindâ€”using mature, natural colors like white,

pumpkin, cool grey and clean, modern fonts that are easy to read. Our

content will be clear and exciting, highlighting both the thrill and safety of the

experience, while calls-to-action should be prominent and friendly to encourage booking

with confidence.
