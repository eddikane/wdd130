Travel & Viewing Guide

Venue Overview

The Allianz Arena in Munich is one of Europe’s premier football stadiums. Opened in 2005, it is home to Bayern Munich and known for its illuminated exterior. For the final, fans will find upgraded services including expanded food zones, enhanced security, and fan-friendly signage in multiple languages.

Travel Tips

Munich is served by Munich International Airport (MUC), located 28 km from the stadium. S-Bahn trains (S1 and S8) connect the airport to central Munich. From there, take U-Bahn Line U6 to the “Fröttmaning” stop, which is a short walk from the arena. Plan early, as the city will be filled with football fans.

Fan Zones & Watch Parties

UEFA has set up fan zones across Munich, including the iconic Marienplatz, with large screens, food, and entertainment. Cities worldwide will host official watch parties so fans everywhere can share the excitement.

Home Viewing Ideas

Bring the UCL experience home! Decorate with team colors, serve themed snacks, and set up a prediction game. UEFA provides printable lineups and activities for kids. Encourage everyone to wear team jerseys and enjoy the atmosphere together.
